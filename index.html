Problem in Healthcare Management

Patients struggle with fragmented healthcare experiences due to difficulties in finding doctors, scheduling appointments, and accessing customer support. Meanwhile, healthcare providers face inefficiencies in managing schedules, analyzing feedback, and handling administrative tasks like paperwork and recruitment. These challenges lead to patient frustration, delayed care, and higher operational costs for clinics, which often require extra staff to manage these inefficiencies.
